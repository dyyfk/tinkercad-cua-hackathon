# Tinkercad Tutorial - How To Make A Small 3D Cat

Source: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=B88Jxg5LQac
Author: Circuits With Angel

This model follows the tutorial in timestamp order, translating each Tinkercad primitive into a local mesh.

## Step 1: Body (00:28)

Add a sphere, scale it to 35 mm wide, 42 mm long, and 20 mm tall.

## Step 2: Eyes (00:41)

Add a 4 mm eye, raise it 10 mm, move it to the front, then copy, paste, and mirror it.

## Step 3: Front legs (00:58)

Use a paraboloid, rotate it 112.5 degrees, scale it to 8 mm wide, 18.95 mm long, and 7.95 mm tall, then mirror the pair.

## Step 4: Back legs (01:33)

Use another paraboloid, rotate it 135 degrees, scale it to 10.02 mm wide, 17.77 mm long, and 12 mm tall, angle it 22.5 degrees, then mirror it.

## Step 5: Ears (02:14)

Use paraboloids scaled to 7 mm wide, 10 mm long, and 8 mm tall, then rotate and mirror them above the eyes.

## Step 6: Tail (02:38)

Use a donut slice, rotate it 90 degrees and 22.5 degrees, then place it at the back of the body.

## Step 7: Nose (02:59)

Use a roof shape scaled to 1 mm wide, 5 mm long, and 1 mm tall, rotate it 90 degrees, and place it between the eyes.
